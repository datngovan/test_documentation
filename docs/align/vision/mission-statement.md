# Mission Statement

> **Owner:** Office of the CEO | **Last Updated:** 2026-01-10

## Our Mission

> *Empower every team to automate what slows them down — so humans can focus on what only humans can do.*

We build AI-native workflow automation infrastructure that turns complex, repetitive processes into reliable, intelligent systems. Our platform sits at the intersection of developer tools and enterprise software: powerful enough for engineers to build anything, accessible enough for business teams to run it themselves.

## Our Vision

In five years, every organization — from a 10-person startup to a 100,000-person enterprise — will run critical business processes on intelligent automation platforms. We intend to be the infrastructure layer beneath that future: the operating system for business logic.

## Why We Exist

Most organizations still automate the way they did in 2010: brittle scripts, manual handoffs, and spreadsheets holding together processes that should never require human attention. The cost is enormous — in wasted time, in errors, in the morale of talented people doing work that should be automated.

We exist to fix that.

## What We Believe

| Belief | What it means in practice |
|--------|--------------------------|
| Automation should be trustworthy | Every execution is logged, auditable, and explainable |
| Developers are our superpower | We build for engineers first — great DX creates enterprise trust |
| AI amplifies humans, not replaces them | Our AI features reduce toil; they don't remove accountability |
| Reliability is a feature | 99.95% uptime isn't a goal, it's a baseline expectation |

## How We Measure Purpose

We know we're succeeding when:

- **Customers tell us** they've eliminated an entire job function worth of manual work
- **Developers advocate** for us internally without being asked
- **Enterprises trust us** with their most sensitive, mission-critical processes
- **Our team is proud** to tell people what they work on
